Pravallika Alluri acts in a play

The Master Builder

Author: Henrik Ibsen

MRS. SOLNESS.
[Turning her head a little.]Have you been round the garden, Miss Wangel?
HILDA.
Yes, I have been taking a look at it.
MRS. SOLNESS.And found some flowers too, I see.
HILDA.Yes, indeed! There are such heaps of them in among the bushes.
MRS. SOLNESS.Are there, really? Still? You see I scarcely ever go there.
HILDA.
[Closer.] What! Don't you take a run down into the garden every day, then?
MRS. SOLNESS.
[With a faint smile.] I don't "run" anywhere, nowadays.
